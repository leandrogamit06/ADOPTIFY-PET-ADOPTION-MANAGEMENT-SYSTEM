The first few days are a period of adjustment for your new pet. Introduce the house rules to your pet through positive reinforcement. Reward good behavior with praise, treats, or their favorite toy.

As your pet settles in, observe their body language such as movement and positioning of the eyes, ears, and tail. Having trouble decoding their movements? Fear not we’ve broken them down here.

Now that you’ve checked everything on this list, we’re confident that you’ll make a great pet owner. The path to fur parenthood might have been tough to navigate, but it is worth every trouble and you’ll know it once your pet finally steps inside your home.
