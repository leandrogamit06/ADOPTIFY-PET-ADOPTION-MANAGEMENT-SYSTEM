WHAT SHOULD I PREPARE BEFORE ADOPTING A PET?

Make sure you have a safe home environment, basic pet supplies, and are ready for the responsibility of caring for your new companion.

WILL THERE BE AN ADOPTION FEE?

Yes, because of the vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and other basic medical care. The fee helps ensure that every adopted pet is healthy and ready for their new home.

WHAT ARE THE REQUIREMENTS FOR ADOPTING A PET?

Each adoption group that lists a pet on Adoptify has its own rules and requirements. Please contact the shelter or group to learn about their process.
